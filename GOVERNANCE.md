# Governance

Stubborn Contract is the official continuation of Spring Cloud Contract, led by
[Marcin Grzejszczak](https://toomuchcoding.com), one of the project's original creators.

- The open-source core remains available under Apache 2.0.
- Community contributions are welcome. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](.github/CONTRIBUTING.md).
- The project evolves at a sustainable pace.
- Major initiatives are funded through Enterprise Partnerships.
- Decisions are made by the project maintainer, informed by community needs and
  Enterprise Partners.
- No public SLA is promised for free/community support.

Full details: https://stubborn.sh/governance

Interested in funding roadmap priorities or getting dedicated support?
See [Enterprise Partnerships](https://stubborn.sh/enterprise).
